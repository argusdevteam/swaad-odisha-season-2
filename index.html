Learn More About Us

Argus News is back with Swaad Odisha Season 3, a one-of-its-kind culinary journey that celebrates the flavors and traditions of our state. After the overwhelming love for Seasons 1 and 2, the new season is bigger, brighter, and more exciting—bringing stories, recipes, and hidden superchefs from every corner of Odisha.

This season will be hosted by Smrutisree Singh, MasterChef India Season 6 finalist, who will guide audiences through authentic dishes, modern twists, and the untold stories behind them. With its vibrant outdoor studio, Swaad Odisha Season 3 is all set to take viewers on a mouth-watering adventure.
